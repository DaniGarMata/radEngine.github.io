General

Camera Controls

  • W - Move forwards
  • A - Move left
  • S - Move backwards
  • D - Move right
  • R - Move up
  • F - Move down
  • Alt+LClick - Rotate
  • Mouse Wheel - Zoom
  • Shift - Double speed
